Detailed Description

The Accumulated class is a special Term that stores a fuzzy set with the Activated terms from the Antecedents of a Rule, thereby serving mainly as the fuzzy output value of the OutputVariables.

The ownership of the activated terms will be transfered to objects of this class, and therefore their destructors will be called upon destruction of this term (or calling Accumulated::clear()).
